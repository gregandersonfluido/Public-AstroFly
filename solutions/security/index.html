Security Use Cases

Critical Infrastructure Protection

Secure power plants, data centers, manufacturing facilities, and other critical infrastructure with comprehensive aerial surveillance. Our drones provide continuous monitoring of large areas that would be impractical to cover with traditional security cameras and sensors.

  • Detect perimeter breaches within seconds
  • Monitor remote areas and blind spots
  • Create a visible deterrent to potential intruders
  • Reduce security personnel exposure to potential threats

Event & Venue Security

Enhance security at large events, concerts, and sports venues with aerial monitoring capabilities. Our drones provide real-time crowd analysis, detect unusual activities, and help security teams respond more effectively to incidents.

  • Monitor crowd flow and identify potential bottlenecks
  • Detect unauthorized access attempts
  • Track persons of interest across large areas
  • Provide aerial support during emergency evacuations

Emergency Response & Law Enforcement

Support first responders and law enforcement with immediate aerial intelligence during emergencies, from natural disasters to security incidents. Our drones can be rapidly deployed to provide crucial situational awareness before ground units arrive.

  • Create accurate mapping of emergency scenes
  • Provide aerial illumination and communication support
